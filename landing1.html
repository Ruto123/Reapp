Conserving and Recycling Plastics

About

Information about the importance of conserving and recycling plastics.

Environmental Conservation: Plastics take hundreds to thousands of years to decompose naturally. Improper disposal of plastics leads to pollution of land, water bodies, and air, endangering wildlife and ecosystems. By conserving and recycling plastics, we reduce the amount of plastic waste that ends up in our environment.
